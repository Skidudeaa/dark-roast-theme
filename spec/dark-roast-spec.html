SECTION 01

The Obsidian Floor

Absolute black (#000000) forces OLED pixels into a full power-off state. Reactivation introduces measurable lag — 18.5ms pixel wake delay — causing visible purple-smearing artifacts during high-speed scrolling.

Black Label establishes a two-tier floor:

#120C06 THE VOID
#160E08 OBSIDIAN ESPRESSO

Both keep pixels energized above the power-off threshold. The Void is the deepest possible floor before smearing risk. Obsidian Espresso is the standard background for content areas.
